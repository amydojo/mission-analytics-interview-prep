# Tell Me About Yourself

## Target length

60 to 90 seconds.

## Recommended answer

> I am a systems and software engineer with a bachelor's in electrical engineering and a master's in statistics. My background has primarily been at the intersection of software, RF systems, automation, and data analysis.
>
> At Raytheon, I developed software for RF simulation and worked in a classified aerospace environment under a Secret clearance. At Viasat, I built Python automation for CI/CD pipelines and created an analytical model using SQL queries of RF test data to predict network traffic. That work helped me identify a defect that was causing traffic to be overestimated by 100%.
>
> I completed my master's in statistics to deepen my analytical and modeling skills. I am interested in this role because it combines the areas where I am strongest: complex system behavior, reliable data, root-cause analysis, and real-world defense applications.

## Delivery notes

- Pause after the Viasat result.
- Do not say "I am trying to transition into data."
- Do not list every language or project.
