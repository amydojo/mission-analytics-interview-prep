# Why Anduril?

## Recommended answer

> My background has already exposed me to defense, RF systems, communications technology, and production engineering. What interests me about Anduril is how closely software and analytics are tied to physical systems operating in real environments.
>
> I am especially drawn to the Air Defense team's focus on detection, tracking, system performance, and rapid deployment. In this role, analytics is not just retrospective reporting. It helps engineering and operational teams understand whether complex systems are performing as expected, identify failures, and make faster decisions. That combination of mission, systems, and data is a strong match for my experience and the direction in which I want to grow.

## Personal truth prompts

- What did he value about working in defense at Raytheon?
- Why does work attached to physical systems appeal to him?

- "Anduril is innovative and growing."
- "I have always been passionate about AI."
- "The products seem cool."
- Generic patriotism Josh cannot defend authentically.
- Repeating the company website with no personal connection.
