## Mission of the team

The Air Defense team develops, tests, deploys, and sustains networked sensors and effectors used across detection, tracking, identification, deterrence, and defeat of unmanned-aircraft threats.

## What the job actually is

This is a hybrid **data engineering + analytics engineering + production systems investigation** role embedded with software, hardware, field operations, product, and leadership.

The person hired will:

- Build and maintain high-quality, low-latency ingestion from multiple source systems.
- Transform structured, semistructured, and unstructured data for operational use.
- Create secure, timely, accurate, trusted, and extensible data models.
- Define reusable entities, workflows, dashboards, and metrics.
- Investigate how deployed systems perform in production.
- Perform root-cause analysis across complex physical and software systems.
- Build analytics solutions for network-isolated and classified environments.
- Improve self-service, documentation, enablement, and developer experience.
- Occasionally travel to build, test, and deploy real-world capabilities.
