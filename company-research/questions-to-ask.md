# Questions to Ask the Recruiter

Choose three.

1. What aspects of my background stood out when the team reviewed my application?
2. How is this position divided between building data infrastructure, investigating system performance, and supporting field operations?
3. What would the next stage of the interview process evaluate?
4. What are the most important problems the person hired into this role would tackle first?
5. How closely does this role work with hardware, autonomy, and field-test teams?
6. How often does the role work in classified or network-isolated environments?
7. How does the team define success for the first six months?

## Best closing question

> Based on our conversation, is there any part of my background that you would like me to clarify before we wrap up?

Use only if delivered calmly, not as a plea for reassurance.
