# Recruiter Phone-Screen Mock

## Instructions for Amy

- Set a timer for 25 minutes.
- Do not coach during an answer.
- Ask one follow-up when an answer is vague.
- Record audio.
- Debrief only after the full mock.

## Questions

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. What interested you in Anduril?
3. Why this Mission Analytics Engineer role?
4. Walk me through the experience most relevant to this position.
5. Tell me more about the RF traffic defect you identified.
6. What did you personally own?
7. How did you validate the result?
8. How would you characterize your experience as data-oriented?
9. What cloud experience do you have?
10. Have you used tools such as Spark, Kafka, Flink, dbt, or Foundry?
11. Why did you leave Viasat?
12. What have you been doing since then?
13. Why are you leaving teaching?
14. What is your current clearance status?
15. Are you comfortable working onsite in Irvine and traveling?
16. What compensation are you targeting?
17. Where are you in your job search?
18. What questions do you have for me?

## Pressure follow-ups

- What was the exact scale?
- What did you personally implement?
- How did you know the data was wrong?
- Why did you choose that method?
- What was the root cause?
- How was the improvement measured?
- What would you do differently now?
- You have not used our exact stack. Why should the team move you forward?
