## Automatic revision triggers

Revise an answer when:

- It exceeds two minutes without a requested deep dive.
- "We" appears without clarifying Josh's ownership.
- A metric is stated without explaining how it was measured.
- A technical term appears without explaining why it mattered.
- The answer does not connect back to the role.
- Josh apologizes for a gap instead of bridging it.
- The listener cannot identify the problem, action, and result.
