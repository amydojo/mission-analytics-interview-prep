# Mock Interview Feedback Log

## Scoring system

Score every answer from 0 to 2 in each category.

- **0:** Missing, inaccurate, or actively harmful
- **1:** Present but vague, unsupported, or poorly delivered
- **2:** Clear, specific, truthful, and persuasive

| Category | What earns a 2 |
|---|---|
| Relevance | Directly answers the question before adding context |
| Specificity | Includes a real system, method, decision, or constraint |
| Ownership | Makes Josh's contribution unmistakable |
| Validation | Explains how he knew the result was correct |
| Role connection | Shows why the evidence matters to mission analytics |
| Clarity | Understandable to the intended interviewer |
| Confidence | Grounded and calm without bluffing |
| Brevity | Appropriate depth without wandering |

Maximum score per answer: **16**

### Readiness thresholds

- **14 to 16:** Ready
- **11 to 13:** Usable, revise once
- **8 to 10:** Weak, rebuild
- **0 to 7:** Unsafe for interview

## Automatic revision triggers

Revise an answer when:

- It exceeds two minutes without a requested deep dive.
- Josh says "we" without defining his ownership.
- A metric appears without a measurement method.
- A technical term appears without explaining why it mattered.
- The answer ends on a weakness rather than a bridge.
- A claim cannot survive one follow-up.
- The story has action but no validation.
- The answer could apply to any company.
- Josh reveals proprietary or classified detail.
